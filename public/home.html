June 17, 2022 by Joy-Ann Gill

Flood Tech Founders Receive Accolades

June 15, 2022 Photo by C. Pitt

The co-founders of Flood Tech, were among some 300 University of the West Indies students and 6000 Barbados citizens who received the National Humanitarian Award on Heroes' Day 2022 for their service to the country during the Covid-19 pandemic.

About

Flood Tech Data Solutions Ltd. was founded in 2019 by two first-year college students in Barbados. Today Flood Tech aims to provide reasonable and efficient technological support to businesses and consumers throughout the Caribbean region.
